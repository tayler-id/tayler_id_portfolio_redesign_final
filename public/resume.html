OnboardIQ, merchant onboarding workflow product (0-to-1)
Codified the entire process for onboarding merchants onto the Versatile platform

Problem: Merchant onboarding ate a full cross functional team. 5 days of meetings per partner to gather configurations from sales, ops, risk, and the partner themselves. Six entity types, each with about 10 metadata fields, arrived from multiple sources via email and Slack threads. Tickets dropped, configs drifted, and stakeholders had no visibility into program status without booking a meeting.
Solution: Designed OnboardIQ from blank canvas. A flexible workflow product that codified the full onboarding process into configurable, partner-aware steps. Unified the 6 entity types and 10 metadata fields into one guided funnel. Built audits and automation directly into the workflow so validation, status, and handoffs were surfaced inline instead of chased over Slack.
Outcome: Onboarded 500 locations for an elective medical practice program in 2 hours. That work previously took 5 days of cross-team meetings. Now powers onboarding for 30,000+ merchants across three verticals (retail, elective medical, home improvement). Support ticket resolution dropped from 3 days to 3 hours. A recurring weekly coordination meeting was replaced by a 5 minute daily brief once audits and automation went live.

  • Owned product design end to end from discovery with sales, ops, risk, and merchants through workflow IA, high fidelity UI, and the iteration after launch.
  • Designed configurable workflow primitives (steps, gates, branching paths) so new partner types onboard without re-designing the product.
  • Unified onboarding data complexity: 6 entity types, 10 metadata fields each, flowing in from multiple partner sources, into one guided funnel that surfaces validation errors at the source instead of catching them three handoffs later.
  • Built audits and automation into the workflow itself, giving stakeholders and program managers live program status without filing a support ticket. Killed the email and Slack thread chase that previously dropped onboarding work.

Consulting Product Designer, Rayni

Designed and shipped Rayni 0-to-1 alongside OnboardIQ at Versatile/Synchrony. Deliberate experience building in AI products and high stakes RAG.
Rayni Platform, AI document intelligence (rayni.ai)
Scientific research labs · in daily production

Problem: Lab technicians couldn't trust AI answers without seeing the source documents. Existing tools provided answers without verification.
Solution: Built a split-screen verification interface with citation deep-linking to exact PDF coordinates so users validate every response. Designed a gap-detection UX (85% confidence threshold) that asks for missing documents instead of guessing. The pattern was later extracted into Document Domain Agents, a domain-agnostic framework for high stakes RAG.
Outcome: Cut document verification time by 60% versus manual PDF search. 92% user compliance with document upload prompts. 40% reduction in false-positive AI responses.

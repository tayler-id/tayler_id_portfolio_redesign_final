Executive Summary

1 The Challenge

OnboardIQ is a comprehensive B2B automation platform that transforms Versatile Credit's manual merchant onboarding process from a 6-month ordeal into a streamlined 2-3 month digital experience. As UX/UI Engineer, I led the complete product development lifecycle—from stakeholder interviews and user journey mapping through full-stack implementation—to solve critical scalability and visibility challenges affecting revenue generation.

"Manual merchant onboarding processes created a 6-month average timeline, preventing scaling beyond current capacity and causing unpredictable revenue forecasting."

Success Metrics Achieved

  • Revenue Acceleration: Faster merchant-to-revenue conversion for $70/location SaaS fee structure across enterprise clients (600+ locations)
  • Operational Scalability: Platform handles volume increases (like Heartland's 800 locations) through automated workflows
  • Process Efficiency: Elimination of 8-hour manual promotional plan configurations through automated bulk import capabilities
  • Knowledge Preservation: Captured tribal knowledge from domain experts into documented workflows, reducing single-point-of-failure dependencies by 90%
  • Communication Consolidation: Replaced fragmented multi-channel communications with centralized platform, reducing coordination overhead by 60%

Process Reflection & Key Learnings

What Made This Project Successful

Comprehensive User Research

15+ stakeholder interviews across all user types revealed systemic issues that surface-level analysis would miss. Card sorting and journey mapping provided actionable insights for platform design.

Enterprise Constraint Navigation

Working within ServiceKit framework, Pebble templates, and Google SSO constraints while delivering modern UX demonstrates sophisticated enterprise development skills.

Business Impact Focus

Every technical decision connected to measurable business outcomes: $2.1M revenue impact, 65% process improvement, 300% capacity scaling.

Technical Innovations

  • Performance-First Animation Framework: Custom hardware-accelerated system with 60fps targets and comprehensive accessibility support
  • Brownfield Enhancement Strategy: Modernizing legacy systems while maintaining full backward compatibility and enterprise stability
  • Multi-Stakeholder UX Design: Single platform serving three distinct user types (Amanda, Snap, Ashel) with competing needs and success metrics
  • Enterprise Integration Patterns: Seamless Salesforce sync, Worth API utilization, and lender system coordination
